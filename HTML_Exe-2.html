Vathal Kuzhambu

Introduction

Vathal Kuzhambu is a popular gravy for rice served all over South India in a traditional meal setting.

Ingredients

  • Gingelly Oil
  • Mustard
  • Fenugreek
  • Cumin
  • Small Onion
  • Garlic
  • Tomato
  • Tamarind water
  • Turkey Berry
  • Brinjal Vathal
  • Mango Vathal
  • Cluster Beans Vathal
  • Chilli Powder
  • Salt

How to make Vathal Kuzhambu

First heat the pan and add 2-3 spoon of Gingelly oil, once heat the oil add all the ingredients mentioned above and fry all the items. Once fried, add Tamarind water, Chilli Powder & Salt. Once boiled everything, very tasty Vathal Kuzhambu is ready.
