Introduction

Last year I've visited Andaman with my husband. My husband planned this trip for my Birthday. Let we will see about Andaman.

Best Places to visit Andaman

  • Cellular Jail (Port Blair)
  • Radha Nagar Beach
  • Ross and North Bay Island
  • Baratang Island
  • Elephant Beach (Havelock Island)

First day we've visited the Cellular Jail. In Cellular Jail, everyday night they conducted the show about our Freedom Fighter. It's one of the most important national monuments from the days of Freedom Struggle.

Second day we've visited Ross Island traveled via Ship. It's a 1st experience in my life to travel in ship. This Island served as a Capital to the British. And its converted into a Prisoner of War (POW) site.

Third day we've visited Havelock Island. Its a famous for Natural Beauty, pristine beaches, and vibrant coral reefs.

Lots of water rides are available in Elephant beach.

Types of Rides

  • Parasailing
  • Snorkeling
  • Jet skiing
  • Sea walking

We've enjoyed above mentioned all the rides, particularly Parasailing is a great experience.

Fourth day we've visited Baratang Island. Baratang is famous for its Natural Wonders from impressive Limestone Caves and dense Mangrove Creeks.

Fifth day we've visited Port Blair includes Chatam Saw Mill, Samudrika Andhropology, Fishers & Sagarika Emporium.
